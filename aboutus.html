Welcome to RGUKT Event Management

Our platform is dedicated to revolutionizing the way events are managed at RGUKT RK Valley. From academic seminars and workshops to cultural festivals and sports competitions, our mission is to streamline the entire event lifecycle, making it easier for students, faculty, and event organizers to plan, promote, execute, and provide feedback on events.

Our Vision

We envision a campus where event management is seamless, efficient, and engaging. By leveraging technology, we aim to enhance collaboration, transparency, and data-driven decision-making in the event planning process.

Our Team

Our team is composed of dedicated students and faculty members from RGUKT RK Valley who are passionate about technology and event management. Together, we bring a diverse set of skills and experiences to create a comprehensive, user-friendly software solution for our campus community.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or feedback, please feel free to reach out to us at eventmanagement@rguktrkvalley.edu.in.
